SHIKAR DHAWAN

Shikhar Dhawan is an Indian international cricketer and a former limited-overs captain.

Being a left-handed opening batsman and an occasional right-arm off break bowler, he plays for Punjab Kings in the Indian Premier League and Delhi in first-class cricket.

Parents: Mahendra Pal Dhawan, Sunaina Dhawan
World Cups: 2019, 2015
